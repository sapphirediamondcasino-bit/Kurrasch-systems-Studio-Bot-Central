About Our Studio

Kurrasch Systems Studios designs advanced, personality-driven bots that connect technology and art. Each project is inspired by emotion, legacy, and community — a way to preserve voices, ideas, and humor. While Game Killers Studios powers some of our systems, every creation here represents collaboration and heart. Diane Keaton’s bot stands as our foundation — free, timeless, and built to honor her presence. From entertainment to utility, our goal is simple: to make AI personal.
