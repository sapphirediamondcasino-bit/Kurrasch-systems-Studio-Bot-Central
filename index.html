About Kurrasch Systems

Kurrasch Systems Studios is the engineering soul of imagination — blending creative direction and technical precision to produce bots that behave like characters, collaborators, and tools. We build for music, events, moderation, and community; we build for heart.

Most of our offerings have optional subscription tiers for advanced features. Diane Keaton’s bot is the single exception — it is completely and forever free. The Systems team powers many bots while partnering studios, such as Game Killer Studio, retain administrative control where appropriate.

Featured Bots

Diane Keaton™

Diane Keaton is our founding character bot — centered around an arcade experience and designed to be warm, nostalgic, and always free. The bot serves as an approachable anchor, welcoming players and guiding them through the memorial arcade experience. Systems provides her technical power; Game Killer Studio holds administrative ownership.

Completely freeArcade-first

Betty White™

Betty White is a community-first assistant focused on engagement and comfort — she runs welcome flows, event prompts, gentle moderation checks, reaction-based games, and light utility features. Designed to make communities feel cared for rather than policed.

Community care

Robin Williams

Robin Williams is our entertainment engine — a fast-thinking persona built for improv, friendly micro-theatre, and interactive prompts. Tunable by server owners, Robin can run quick improv games, deliver uplifting monologues, and help host live community events.

Improv & entertainment

Stan’s Beat Bot™

Stan’s Beat Bot is made for creators — a lightweight beat-hosting and preview system with community feedback channels, collaborative sessions, and demo drops. Built by music people, Stan simplifies sharing and critique in producer communities.

Music & collaboration

NHI Conference™

NHI Conference coordinates large-scale events — speaker queues, session rooms, reminders, and agenda management. Designed to replicate conference flows inside Discord while keeping attendee communication clear and human.

Events & scheduling

NHI™

NHI is the Systems toolkit for integrations and automation — webhooks, external data syncs, role-syncs, and routine automations that keep busy servers running without manual work.

Integrations

KS Application Bot™

KS Application Bot runs multi-step application forms, applicant tracking, and staff review workflows. It exports records, timestamps decisions, and helps keep onboarding organized for studios and volunteer teams.
